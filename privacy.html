🎬 SnapSum Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

1. Introduction

SnapSum ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Screen Recordings

When you use SnapSum to record your screen:

  • Video Data: Screen recordings are processed locally in your browser. We do NOT upload or store your recordings on our servers.
  • Audio Data: If you enable microphone/audio, audio is processed for transcription only. Audio is sent to our AI provider (SiliconFlow) for speech-to-text conversion and immediately discarded after processing.

2.2 AI Summary Generation

To generate summaries of your recordings:

  • Transcribed text is sent to SiliconFlow API (Qwen AI model) for summary generation.
  • We do NOT store the transcribed text or summaries on our servers.
  • All AI processing uses enterprise-grade API with data retention policies.

2.3 Usage Statistics

We store locally in your browser:

  • Number of recordings this month (for free tier limit)
  • Your subscription status (free/pro)
  • License key (if you've upgraded)

This data never leaves your device.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 SiliconFlow AI API

We use SiliconFlow for:

  • Speech-to-text (FunAudioLLM/SenseVoiceSmall)
  • Text summarization (Qwen2.5-7b-instruct)

SiliconFlow's privacy policy: https://siliconflow.cn/privacy

4. Data Storage

  • All recordings are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API.
  • Recordings are automatically deleted when you close the extension or manually clear browser data.
  • We have NO access to your recordings or summaries.

5. User Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Download: You can download your recordings and summaries at any time.
  • Delete: Recordings are deleted when you close the result view. You can also uninstall the extension to remove all data.
  • Opt-out: You can choose not to enable microphone recording.

6. Children's Privacy

SnapSum is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Security

  • All communication with AI APIs uses HTTPS encryption.
  • API keys are embedded in the extension and not exposed to users.
  • Screen capture requires explicit permission each time you record.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users via the Chrome Web Store update notes.
